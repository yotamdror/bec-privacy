BEC App

Privacy Policy

The short version

BEC uses your location to find the nearest bacon, egg & cheese, pizza, and bagel. That's it. No account, no tracking, no data sold.

Location

BEC requests access to your location while the app is in use. Your coordinates are sent to our server solely to look up nearby places and calculate walking directions. We do not store your location, log it, or associate it with any identifier.

Location access is required for the app to function. If you deny or revoke permission, you can re-enable it in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → BEC.

Third-party services

Place search and walking directions are powered by Google APIs, accessed through a server-side proxy. Your location is passed to Google solely to fulfill these requests. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.

When you tap the map to navigate, you are handed off to Apple Maps or Google Maps. Their respective privacy policies apply at that point.

Purchases

The optional "Buy me a coffee" tip is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Feedback

If you use the in-app feedback option, it opens a pre-addressed email in your mail app. No data is sent unless you choose to send the email yourself.

Children

BEC is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
